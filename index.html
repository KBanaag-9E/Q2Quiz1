José Rizal

José Protacio Rizal Mercado y Alonso Realonda

June 1861 — 30 December 1896

About the Hero

  • Born in Calamba, Luzon
  • Executed in Manila, Philippines
  • Founded "La Liga Filipina"
  • Worked as a doctor, intellectual, and patriot
  • Known as a Philippine National Hero
